Why This Site Exists

This site was born out of frustration. As someone with actual professional training in the very subjects that conspiracy theorists misrepresent — continuity of government, emergency management, federal continuity directives — watching people like Jon Herold and Derek Johnson mislead hundreds of thousands of Americans was unacceptable.

These content creators take real government documents and misinterpret them, either through ignorance or deliberate deception, to build narratives that:

  • Undermine trust in the Constitution and democratic institutions
  • Keep patriots passive by telling them to "trust the plan" instead of participating in governance
  • Generate revenue through paid subscriptions, merchandise, and speaking tours
  • Produce results identical to what foreign adversaries want — Americans distrusting their own government

This site provides the counterargument that was missing. Every claim is sourced. Every document cited is linked. The actual government frameworks are explained by someone who studied them professionally. And it's all free.
